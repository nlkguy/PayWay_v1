PayWay Solutions ("Company", "we", "us", "our") operates the PayWay Solutions website (the "Service"). 2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Information Collected:

  • Personal Data: Personal identification information including but not limited to name, email address, phone number, and address

PayWay Solutions uses the collected data for various purposes:

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We implement the following security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit

  • Secure password policies and multi-factor authentication

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 18 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18.
